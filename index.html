The Activity Generator 3000

Description

If you are feeling bored, simply select the category, amount of money you care to spend, and the amount of people you you want to do this with, then click the "Generate" button! You will be introduced to a new random activity, along with a Youtube video teaching you how to do said activity. If you really like a choice then you can add the choice to your favorites, by clicking the favorites button, so that you can view the video later.
